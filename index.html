The Biden Connection

Rehoboth Beach ‚Äî formerly "The Presidential Retreat"

Former President Biden & Rehoboth Beach

Joe and Jill Biden purchased their Rehoboth Beach home in the North Shores community in 2017 for $2.7 million. The six-bedroom Cape Cod-style residence at 32 Far View Road served as the presidential retreat throughout Biden's term, putting Rehoboth in the international spotlight.
