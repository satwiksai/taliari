01

Boutique Luxury · 2 Blocks from Boardwalk

The Bellmoor Inn & Spa

6 Christian Street · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

The crown jewel of Rehoboth's boutique hotel scene. Recently awarded Best Designed Hotel in Delaware by House Beautiful, The Bellmoor balances coastal elegance with five-star service. With 78 rooms across several buildings, an award-winning full-service spa, two seasonal pools, gorgeous gardens, and a daily hot breakfast included, it's the most amenity-rich option on our list. The Club Level offers adults-only exclusivity with evening hors d'oeuvres and cocktails.

Boutique Luxury · On Rehoboth Avenue

Hotel Rehoboth

247 Rehoboth Avenue · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

A polished boutique property on Rehoboth's main avenue with impeccable service. The 52 oversized rooms and suites are among the most spacious in town, with luxurious bedding and coastal décor. The standout perk: a nightly wine and cheese social hour, turn-down service with chocolates, and a made-to-order hot breakfast every morning at Lupo Italian Kitchen. The open-air beach shuttle includes complimentary chairs and towels — just show up and relax.

#1 TripAdvisor · 1 Block from Beach

The Avenue Inn & Spa

33 Wilmington Avenue · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

The #1-rated hotel in Rehoboth Beach on TripAdvisor — and for good reason. Just one block from the beach and boardwalk, The Avenue Inn pairs an exceptional full-service spa (with sauna, steam room, and outdoor hot tub) with The Blue Hen, an award-winning restaurant serving seasonal American cuisine. The heated saltwater pool, beach shuttle, and intimate boutique atmosphere make this a top-tier pick for a refined family getaway.

04

Victorian Oceanfront · ON the Boardwalk

Boardwalk Plaza Hotel

2 Olive Avenue & the Boardwalk · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

The grande dame of Rehoboth Beach. This 84-room Victorian oceanfront hotel sits directly on the boardwalk with sweeping ocean views from most rooms. Victoria's Restaurant serves breakfast, brunch, lunch, and dinner with Atlantic views. The Concierge Level unlocks an exclusive rooftop sundeck with two hot tubs. Don't miss the afternoon tea service and the charming resident parrots in the lobby. Room service, whirlpool suites, and 2-bedroom apartments are available.

05

Oceanfront · ON the Boardwalk

Atlantic Sands Hotel & Conference Center

1 Baltimore Avenue · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

A classic oceanfront family hotel right on the boardwalk with direct beach access. The Atlantic Sands has been a Rehoboth institution for decades, recently modernized with updated rooms and amenities. Kiwi's Kove restaurant serves beachfront dining, and the Tiki Hut offers poolside drinks with ocean views. The outdoor pool overlooks the Atlantic. Note: breakfast is not included, but Kiwi's Kove and dozens of nearby restaurants have you covered. Great value for an oceanfront position.

06

Modern Boutique · Tapestry Collection by Hilton

Coast Rehoboth Beach

2 Rehoboth Avenue · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

The newest upscale boutique hotel in Rehoboth, part of Hilton's Tapestry Collection. Coast offers 60 sleek, modern rooms with coastal styling, Keurig coffee makers with local Rise Up coffee, wet bars with mini fridge and microwave, and luxurious bathrobes. Sirocco restaurant serves lunch and dinner downstairs. The rooftop features an indoor saltwater pool and an outdoor patio with a firepit — perfect for evening drinks. Hilton Honors points accepted. Note: breakfast is available at Sirocco but not included in the rate.

07

Family & Pet-Friendly · Near Silver Lake

The Breakers Hotel & Suites

105 2nd Street · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

A long-standing Rehoboth favorite with 98 elegantly decorated rooms and suites set near Silver Lake. The Breakers offers a year-round heated rooftop indoor pool, a cozy reading lounge, and complimentary continental breakfast (served at sister hotel two blocks away). One- and two-bedroom suites feature balconies, microwaves, and separate living areas. One of the few dog-friendly hotels in town with 15 dedicated pet rooms. Beach chairs and umbrellas are complimentary for guests.

08

Beach-Adjacent · 60 Feet from the Sand

Admiral on Baltimore

2 Baltimore Avenue · Rehoboth Beach, DE 19971

You can't get much closer to the beach without sleeping on the sand. The Admiral sits just 60 feet from Rehoboth Beach with ocean views from nearly every room. This family-owned hotel recently completed a major renovation with all new flooring, furniture, mattresses, and flat-screen TVs. The indoor pool features a glass ceiling for natural light and connects to a rooftop sundeck. Every room has a microwave, fridge, and coffee maker. No on-site restaurant or breakfast, but you're steps from dozens of options on the boardwalk.
